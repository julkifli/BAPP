How to Apply

Get ready for your admission. We accept applications twice a year during the main academic intakes.

01

Check Entry Requirements

Use our interactive Course Matchmaker above or view the official requirements handbook to see if you match the courses of your choice.

02

Prepare SPM Results & Certificates

Get soft copies of your SPM slip, identity card, school leaving certificate, and co-curricular records ready for online upload.

03

Submit Application via UPU

Fill out the application online at the Ministry of Higher Education portal (UPUOnline). Select your top Politeknik choices.

04

Offer Acceptance & Enrollment

Once selected, download your official offer letter, complete the medical report, pay the affordable tuition fees online, and register at your assigned campus.
